Step into a dark reality

In a reality where everything functions abnormally, find yourself in a virtual world where you navigate through obstacles in reverse

Insight of the game

In this surreal environment, players will quickly discover that their instincts can no longer be trusted, as every action leads to unexpected outcomes.

Movements

Navigate through the maze in the dark filled with creatures awaiting their next meal. Use the WASD keys to move around the maze and collect coins.

Goal

This game is a test of your ability to navigate through the darkness, and to find the golden beam. You have to collect 5 coins to get a power ability to either give you a speed boost, brighten your flashlight to be more aware of your surroundings or teleport closer to the end goal.
